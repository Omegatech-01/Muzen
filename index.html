FAQ & Help

Got Questions? We have answers.

Muzen is 100% free with no subscription, sign-ups, or hidden fees!

Yes, absolutely! Muzen is built to be a free-for-all creative environment. There are no credit cards, hidden subscriptions, or watermark fees. You can generate unlimited tracks using both Muzen-1.0 and Muzen-2.0 models.

Muzen-1.0 specializes in high-fidelity catchy tracks. Muzen-2.0 supports detailed parameters (selecting specific genres, moods, vocals, and tempo values) as well as an advanced Custom/Manual mode where you can input custom lyrics and titles.

Muzen features a real-time Background Generations Queue. After triggering a song, you can immediately change parameters and click generate again. The songs will process in the background, and you'll receive a toast notification and a prompt when they finish!
